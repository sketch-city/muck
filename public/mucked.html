If your home is mucked or you're a volunteer, tap on the map to mark your location!

Texas Muck Map aims to make it simpler for people with flooded homes to coordinate with nearby volunteers. Zoom into your current location on the map and tap on it to drop a custom pin. Please include a phone number and any extra information in the description to help locate and identify you.

If you have a flooded home:

Pin your location to let volunteers know where you are and make it easier for them to find you.

If you are a volunteer:

Pin your location to let people in the area know you are available. Find people who have marked themselves as needing help.
